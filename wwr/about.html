Extreme Current Adventures

Extreme Current Adventures delivers safe, thrilling whitewater rafting, respecting nature and guiding unforgettable river memories through expert leadership and conservation.

History

Extreme Current Adventures started in 1998 when a small group of passionate river guides set out with two rafts and a dream to share the excitement of wild rivers. Over the past two decades, we have grown into a premier outdoor adventure company, guiding thousands of families, adrenaline seekers, and corporate teams down some of the most scenic and thrilling whitewater stretches in the country.
